About the program

Athletics program for Topcon and Sokkia total stations

Supported total station series:

  • AT: Topcon GPT, QS
  • AT-PS: Topcon PS, OS - Sokkia SX - 320 x 240
  • AT-GT: Topcon GT - Sokkia IX - 640 x 480

The program offers:

  • Measurement of athletic disciplines:
    • long jump, triple jump
    • javelin throw
    • discus throw
    • hammer throw
    • shot put
    • pole vault, high jump
  • Option to export data online with output selection
  • Saving results to logs
  • Graphical display of results
